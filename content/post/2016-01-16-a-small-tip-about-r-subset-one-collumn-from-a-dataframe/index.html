For example:

temp <- data.frame(x = c(1, 2, 4), y = c(34, 23, 45))

method 1. temp[1] get a data frame

method 2. temp[, 1] get a vector

Below is R code and output.

temp
##   x  y
## 1 1 34
## 2 2 23
## 3 4 45
str(temp)
## 'data.frame':    3 obs. of  2 variables:
##  $ x: num  1 2 4
##  $ y: num  34 23 45
temp[1]
##   x
## 1 1
## 2 2
## 3 4
str(temp[1])
## 'data.frame':    3 obs. of  1 variable:
##  $ x: num  1 2 4
temp[, 1]
## [1] 1 2 4
str(temp[, 1])
##  num [1:3] 1 2 4
